🐸 Miss Ale – English Challenge

Learn English the fun way with mini-games designed to practice vocabulary, spelling, and reading comprehension.

v3.0.0

🎮 Available Games

Word Scramble — Unscramble letters to form the correct English word. Use Custom mode to add your own words.

Reading Quest — Read a teacher-provided passage and answer 5 comprehension questions. No going back during the quiz!

Coming soon: Memory Match, Spelling Bee, Hangman, Quiz...

📋 Teacher Mode

In Word Scramble, choose Custom to build your own word list with hints.

In Reading Quest, paste any text, add an image URL, and write 5 multiple-choice questions.

👥 Multiplayer

Play solo or compete with up to 10 friends. Most correct answers wins — ties broken by total time.
